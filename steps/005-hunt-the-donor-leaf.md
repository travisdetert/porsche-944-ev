# Step 005 — Hunt the donor Leaf (G1)

**Phase:** BUY · **Task:** #5 · **Gate:** G1 · **Cost:** ~$2,500–4,500 (the big buy)
**Blocked by:** 001 (PF1 — confirm the year is supported)
**Blocks:** 006, 007

## Do
- [ ] Target a **2013–2017 EM57** Leaf (salvage/wrecked or battery-degraded).
- [ ] Run **LeafSpy** before buying: record **SOH (> 70 %)**, cell-pair spread, charge counts.
- [ ] Match crash damage to parts you **don't** need (front hit = bad; rear/side = usually OK).
- [ ] Don't overpay to rush — keep 2–3 listings warm.

## Done when
Donor bought; LeafSpy SOH recorded; price reflects pack health.

## Refs
`../docs/phase1-donor-hunt.md` · ADR-0001

## Notes
- A **running** donor cuts the commissioning gremlin phase dramatically — worth paying for.

## Tools
- LeafSpy Pro + an ELM327 OBD dongle
- The donor vetting checklist (`../docs/mvp-sourcing.md`)
- A broker/membership for Copart/IAAI if needed

## Time & difficulty
days–weeks (hunting) · moderate

## Tips & gotchas
- Target **2018+ 40 kWh, SOH > 85 %**, pack undamaged.
- Buy the **whole car** — you want the charge port, pedal, PDM, LBC and harness too.
- If you can, **LeafSpy the pack before bidding**; photos rarely show pack health.
- Front-end damage is fine; **rear/side impact risks the pack**.

## Avoid
- Water-damaged cars (HV + water = scrap).
- Salvage with airbag/pack-area impact.
