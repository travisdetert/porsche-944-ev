# Step 005 — Hunt the donor Leaf (G1)

**Phase:** BUY · **Task:** #5 · **Gate:** G1 · **Cost:** ~$2,500–4,500 (the big buy)
**Blocked by:** 001 (PF1 — confirm the year is supported)
**Blocks:** 006, 007

## Do
- [ ] Target a **2013–2017 EM57** Leaf (salvage/wrecked or battery-degraded).
- [ ] Run **LeafSpy** before buying: record **SOH (> 70 %)**, cell-pair spread, charge counts.
- [ ] Match crash damage to parts you **don't** need (front hit = bad; rear/side = usually OK).
- [ ] Don't overpay to rush — keep 2–3 listings warm.

## Done when
Donor bought; LeafSpy SOH recorded; price reflects pack health.
