# Step 010 — Mount the motor; verify driveline true (G3)

**Phase:** BUILD · **Task:** #10 · **Gate:** G3 · **Cost:** ~$60–150 (mounts)
**Blocked by:** 009 (adapter fits)
**Blocks:** 011

## Do
- [ ] Bolt the coupler to the EM57 and the adapter plate to the bellhousing.
- [ ] Fabricate/secure **motor mounts to the factory crossmember hardpoints** (not sheetmetal).
- [ ] **Check runout** with a dial indicator; rotate the driveline by hand.
- [ ] Plumb the inverter/motor **coolant loop** (reuse the Leaf pump).

## Done when
Motor mounted; driveline **turns smoothly by hand, runout in spec, no bind**.

## Refs
`../docs/build-guide.md` §3 · `../images/step-3-motor-mounted.svg`

## Notes
- **Do NOT proceed to the pack until this is green** — fixing alignment is cheap now, agony later.

## Tools
- Dial indicator
- Bubble/torque level
- Poly/rubber mount bushings
- Welder or bolt-in bracket stock

## Time & difficulty
1–2 days · moderate

## Tips & gotchas
- Mount so motor + adapter + torque tube share **one axis** (app Driveline view).
- **Rotate the driveline by hand** — it should turn smooth and true, no bind.
- Mounts tie to **factory crossmember hardpoints, never sheetmetal**.
- Bushings isolate motor/gear whine — firm enough to still locate.

## Avoid
- Any angular/parallel misalignment — it eats bearings and buzzes.
- Welding to thin floor pan.
