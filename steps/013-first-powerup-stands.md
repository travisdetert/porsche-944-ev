# Step 013 — First power-up on stands (G6)

**Phase:** DRIVE · **Task:** #13 · **Gate:** G6 · **Cost:** ~$0
**Blocked by:** 012
**Blocks:** 014

## Do
- [ ] Follow the **`../SECURITY.md` first-power-up protocol**: car on stands, extinguisher,
      helper at a kill switch, one-hand rule.
- [ ] Insert MSD → key on → watch **precharge → main** (no chatter/smoke/faults).
- [ ] Spin the motor under **light throttle**; verify **rotation direction**, both directions, regen.
- [ ] Watch pack voltage/current/temps.

## Done when
Correct rotation, smooth driveline, stable temps, **no faults** — under power, on stands.

## Refs
**`../SECURITY.md`** · `../docs/build-guide.md` §6A

## Notes
- Anything wrong → **kill power, walk away, diagnose de-energized.** Never "just try it again" live.
