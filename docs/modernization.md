# Bringing a 1987 944 Up to Date

The EV swap is the big modernization. Beyond it, here's what's worth adding — and what keeps
the soul. *Modernize the experience, not the character.*

## Already in the plan
- **Touchscreen head unit** — maps, telemetry, drive modes, trip logging (`app/`)
- **Reuse-the-dash** with EV-repurposed gauges (ADR-0012)
- **Stereo + subwoofers** (ADR-0009) · **electric power steering** (ADR-0013)

## Easy modern upgrades
- **Backup camera** (USB cam → head unit) + parking sensors
- **LED lighting** — head/tail/interior (**keep the pop-ups!**)
- **Keyless / phone entry**; **remote pre-conditioning** (warm/cool the cabin while plugged in)
- **Heated seats** (cheap 12 V kits) · **USB-C charge ports** · **dashcam** (the head unit records)

## Drivetrain-enabled (the VCU gives these almost for free)
- **One-pedal driving** + configurable regen · **drive modes** (done)
- **Traction / launch control** via the inverter · **hill-hold**, creep tuning
- **Telemetry + persistent trip logging** (done) → validates the range model

## The fun stuff (head-unit eye candy)
- **3D model of the car with animated pop-up headlights** — the 944's signature — **built! see
  the CAR tab.** Toggle the pop-ups; brake lights glow on regen; the charge port glows when charging.
- Ambient interior lighting tied to drive mode · animated boot/shutdown · valet/track themes
- Live "car status" overlay (lights, doors, handbrake) from the body inputs (`data-sources.md`)

## Keep the soul
Pop-up headlights, the analog-look dash option, the raw stripped feel, the 80s wedge. An electric
heart in an icon — not a generic EV. *Light, low, loud, and unmistakably a 944.*
